Cheetah

The average length of a cheetah run is under a minute, with a distance of between 200 and 300 meters.

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average Speed: 80 – 130 km/h
  • Average Weight: 21 – 72 kg
  • Average Lifespan: About 12 years

The cheetah is the speediest terrestrial mammal on the planet among the most vulnerable big cat in Africa. The cheetah is particularly designed for quickness and can accelerate from 0 to 110 km/h in less than three seconds. Their foot is 7 meters in length when moving at peak speed.
